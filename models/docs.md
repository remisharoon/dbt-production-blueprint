Total Revenue is the sum of net order totals after accounting for soft-deleted orders and currency normalization assumptions. Use this metric for executive reporting and finance reconciliations.

{% docs ltv %}
Lifetime Value (LTV) represents the cumulative net revenue attributed to a customer across all orders. It is commonly used for cohort analysis, retention strategy, and marketing ROI.
